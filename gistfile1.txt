// pseudocode for doing Laundry
1.	Get all dirty clothes from Laundry Basket. 
2.	Put all clothes in Washing machine. 
3.	Add Washing Liquid to washing machine. 
4.	Select a washing program on washing machine.
5.	Turn on the washing machine
6.	Set timer in case washing machine does not have timer to alert you of its time of finish
7.	Get Clothes from washing machine after done. 
8.	Leave clothes to dry in Balcony until dry. 
9.	Gather dried clothes
10.	Put them back in wardrobe/drawyers
